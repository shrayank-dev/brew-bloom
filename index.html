Our Story

A small corner of the city, brewed slowly.

Brew Bloomed started as a love letter to slow mornings. We were two friends tired of rushed coffee and louder cafés — so we built the place we wanted to walk into: warm light, soft music, beans roasted a few streets away, and syrups we make ourselves from the botanicals in our garden.

Six years later, we're still doing the same thing — one cup, one bloom, one good morning at a time.

Mira & Theo
Co-founders Roasters, gardeners, regulars.

Why Brew Bloomed

The little things we obsess over.

Good coffee is mostly the boring stuff, done carefully — for years. Here's what that looks like in our shop.

Roasted in-house

Every bean we serve is roasted within 7 days, in our small drum two streets over.

Garden botanicals

Lavender, rose, mint & cardamom — grown in our backyard, never from a bottle.

Slow by design

We don't do wifi for everyone. We do comfy chairs, soft music, and time to think.

Made with care

Fair-trade beans, living wages for our team, and pastries from the bakery next door.
